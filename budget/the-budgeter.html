Connect Google Sheets

One-time setup — takes about 10 minutes. Your budget data will sync automatically across all devices.

Step 1 — Create the SheetGo to sheets.google.com, create a new spreadsheet, and name it Budget History.
Step 2 — Open Apps ScriptIn the Sheet, click Extensions → Apps Script. Delete any existing code.
Step 3 — Paste the scriptCopy the budget-script.gs file (included in your download) and paste it into the editor. Click Save (disk icon).
Step 4 — DeployClick Deploy → New deployment. Set type to Web app. Set Execute as to Me and Who has access to Anyone. Click Deploy, then Authorize access and follow the prompts.
Step 5 — Copy the URLAfter deploying, copy the Web app URL (looks like https://script.google.com/macros/s/…/exec) and paste it below.
